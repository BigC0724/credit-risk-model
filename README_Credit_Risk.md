
# Credit Risk Model

## Overview
A machine learning model that predicts whether a loan 
applicant will default or repay based on their financial 
profile. Built using real world lending criteria from 
the consumer finance industry.

## Results
- Overall Accuracy: 90%
- Default Recall: 86%
- Good Loan Approval Rate: 90%
- Defaults Correctly Caught: 310 out of 361

## Business Impact
- Potentially protected $1.5M to $7.75M in loan losses
- Only wrongly rejected 9.7% of good borrowers
- Model decisions align with industry underwriting standards

## Features Used
- Credit Score
- Debt To Income Ratio
- Income
- Employment Years
- Loan Amount
- Age

## Key Finding
Debt To Income Ratio (37%) and Credit Score (31%) were 
the strongest predictors of default — accounting for 
68% of the model's decision making.
